Starting today Manifold Studio is open to the public. Anyone can now deploy and own their own Manifold Creator contract and mint ERC-721 and ERC-1155 NFTs. Over the last two months we’ve been able to see how Manifold Studio has been leveraged to create unique experiences with a seamless user interface that required zero coding.
