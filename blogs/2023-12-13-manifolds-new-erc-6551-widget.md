Manifold introduces a display and interaction widget for ERC-6551 Token Bound Accounts (TBAs), built in collaboration with FEWOCiOUS for the FewoWorld Dressing Room. The widget lets NFTs control their own smart contract accounts and offers customizable layouts and developer callbacks.
